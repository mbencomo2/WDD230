Target Audience

Our website aims to assist members of both our chamber and our local community. From businesses seeking membership, to community representatives who want to know about upcoming events, we want to ensure people can easily find the information they need to be the most productive and achieve the best results for our businesses and consumers.

Our primary target audience are the businesses owners and representatives of our local area. This means our website should reflect events, news, and information relavant to these visitors first and foremost. Additionally, we have consumers who wish to obtain information on our members for networking or journalism. As our memebers are top priority, we will make finding contact information and event information as simple as possible.
