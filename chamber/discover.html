Anchorage

Standing at 61° North, Anchorage Alaska sits slightly farther north than Oslo, Stockholm, Helsinki, and Saint Petersburg while being as far west as Honolulu, Hawaii.

The average temperature varies between 68°F during the 4 summer months and 13°F during the coldest part of the year.

Yearly precipitation is around 17.3 inches.

Demographics

Anchorage is one of the largest cities in the US encompassing nearly 1,961 square miles and serving around300000 people.

The city serves as a major source of crude oil and a central transportation hub for moving goods across the globe.

Look for Moose!

Moose can be found in the area all year round, and the population can reach as many as 1500 moose!

Take the Train

Trains are used to transport goods and people across the state. They travel through beautiful mountain scenery. Take a ride!

Visit a Museum

Learn more about the natural history of the area and how the city came to become one of the most important trade routes on the globe.

Ride a Tramway

A tram travels suspended by a cable high throught the mountains. Take in the views high above the trees as you overlook the bay!

Taste Alaska

Anchorage is famous for its seafood, especially tasty crustaceans such as lobster and crab.
